Hiring-manager conversation

Scale the work. Not just the prompt.

Role objective. Convert growing enterprise AI availability into secure, testable, reusable internal workflows that improve productivity while preserving decision authority, risk controls, and adoption.

Public company moment

Platform availabilityPNC reported deployment of multiple generative-AI platforms, including Microsoft Copilot.
Data foundationPublic reporting describes data-domain strategies intended to accelerate value while mitigating risk.
Responsible AIPNC publicly emphasizes accountability, security, privacy, model validation, monitoring, and human involvement.

Central tensions

TensionWhat the role must make explicit
Speed vs. controlData boundary, permission, proof, fallback, and decision owner.

Reuse vs. process specificityWhat is a shared pattern and what remains local context or exception logic.

My interview thesis

A production prompt is not merely an instruction. It is one component in a bounded workflow containing context, permissions, tools, evaluation, security controls, human authority, telemetry, ownership, and measurable outcomes.

Technical discussion

The AI Work Control Envelope

1 · Business intent

Define the completed outcome, process owner, user, baseline friction, consequence, and acceptable boundary.

2 · Approved context

Identify source ownership, access, freshness, data classification, retrieval behavior, and no-answer conditions.

3 · Tool authority

Set least privilege, allowlisted actions, identity inheritance, reversibility, limits, and escalation.

4 · Evaluation evidence

Test task success, groundedness, completeness, edge cases, refusal, injection, permissions, and regression.

5 · Human decision

Name approval, exception, override, accountability, and when the system must stop or ask.

6 · Operating record

Observe usage, completed outcomes, edits, overrides, failures, recovery, cost, adoption, and source gaps.

Likely technical questions and answer spine

QuestionAnswer spine
How do you know a prompt is production-ready?Versioned prompt + representative test set + expected behavior + groundedness/completeness + refusal/edge cases + regression + named owner.
How do you secure an agent?Classify data; inherit identity; use least-privilege tools; defend against direct/indirect injection; validate outputs; require human approval for consequential actions; log the complete action record.
How do SOLID principles apply?One bounded purpose per component; stable contracts among orchestration, retrieval, tools, and models; replaceable providers; small tool interfaces; business logic depending on abstractions.
How do you measure value?Baseline labor, queue delay, cycle time, rework, error/exception rate, risk exposure, and adoption—then measure the changed workflow and completed outcome, not prompt volume.
How hands-on are you?Direct workflow design, prompts, structured context, agent patterns, evaluation, governance, operating measures, Python/JavaScript-adjacent implementation, and precise collaboration with deeper application specialists.

Strong example structure

Vape-Jet issue-to-resolution workflow

Inputs across HubSpot, Jira, Slack, Odoo, telemetry, and field information create incomplete intake and slow handoffs. Structure context, check missing information, classify and route, preserve engineering/quality/safety judgment, and measure intake delay, completeness, duplicate touches, backlog age, recurring failures, resolution time, and user acceptance.

Hands-on value profile

Lead with direct workflow, operating, prompt, agent, evaluation, and governance work. Make architectural boundaries, specialist collaboration, test evidence, and operational ownership precise. Precision is a strength in a control-sensitive environment.

Executive questions

  1. When you say “scale prompts,” what is the primary unit of scale: more users, more processes, reusable engineering patterns, or an internal platform capability?
  2. How does work move today from a process owner’s idea through engineering, quality, security, and approval into an operating workflow?
  3. Which part of that path creates the most friction today: requirements, data access, evaluation, controls, release, or adoption?
  4. What decisions does this role own directly, and where is it expected to influence through architecture or partnership?
  5. What would make you say after 90 days that this person is materially changing how the organization uses AI?
  6. What does five-day onsite collaboration enable for this team that would be difficult to accomplish remotely?

Interview rules

  • No AI assistant, teleprompter, or outside help during the live interview.
  • Use one primary story deeply rather than several shallow examples.
  • When a fact is not known, label it as a discovery question rather than improvising certainty.
  • Close with the operating thesis and one calibrated question about the team’s real scale objective.
