# PNC Enterprise Engineer Sr – AI Candidate Campaign

An independent candidate vision by Russell Dudek for PNC requisition 71280.

The campaign argues that enterprise AI should scale complete, controlled workflows rather than isolated prompts. Its central candidate-created mechanism, the AI Work Control Envelope, binds business intent, approved context, tool authority, evaluation evidence, human decision rights, and an operating record around each AI-assisted workflow.

## Artifact set

- Candidate vision
- Two-page role-aligned resume
- One-page cover letter
- Three-page interview thesis brief
- Two-page 90-day entry plan
- Two-page AI Work Control Review

## Sources and evidence

Company and role intelligence is grounded in public PNC reporting, PNC’s public Responsible AI and careers materials, the supplied requisition information, and verified candidate evidence. Public hypotheses are labeled; no internal PNC process, architecture, reporting relationship, or maturity claim is implied.

The PNC logo is used unmodified for nominative employer identification. The campaign is clearly labeled as independent candidate work and does not imply PNC sponsorship or endorsement.
